Frequently Asked Questions

Why do small websites need ADA & Equality Act compliance? ▼
In the USA, courts rule that websites are public spaces under Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). In the UK, the Equality Act 2010 mandates that service providers ensure equal access. Non-compliant sites are major targets for lawsuit settlement firms demanding thousands of dollars in easy out-of-court settlements.
